How the affiliate program works

Refer dealerships to MarketSync and earn 8% of everything they pay, for the life of the customer. Here's the whole thing, step by step.

1Grab your link

Copy your referral link at the top of this page. It has your code baked in — anyone who signs up through it is credited to you automatically, forever.

2Share it

Send it to dealers you know, post it, or add it to your email signature. No cap on how many you refer — commission stacks.

3Track your referrals

The Referrals table shows each dealership and its status: Trialing (signed up, in free trial), Active/Paying (subscription is live — you're earning), or inactive.

4Watch it add up

Every time a referred dealer pays, 8% is added to your Commissions. It moves from Pending → Approved → Paid.

💡 Read your four tiles: Referrals (total signed up) · Paying (live subscriptions earning you money) · Pending (earned, not yet paid out) · Lifetime earned (all-time).
5Get paid

Set your Payout email at the bottom of the dashboard — that's where the MarketSync team sends your money. Keep it current.

6Sell it in one line

“It's every dealership tool — website, CRM, pricing, Facebook, deals, service, books — in one login, with a 30-day free trial.” Send them your link to start.

Questions about a payout or your account? Email hello@marketsync.link.
