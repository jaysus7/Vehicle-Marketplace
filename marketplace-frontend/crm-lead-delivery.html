What is CRM lead delivery?

MarketSync speaks the language automotive CRMs already understand. When a shopper messages you about a vehicle, you log the lead and MarketSync sends a standard ADF/XML lead — the format virtually every automotive CRM ingests — straight to your CRM's intake email, with the vehicle and customer details attached. Your Marketplace leads land in the same place as the rest of your pipeline, so nothing falls through the cracks.

Frequently asked questions

What format are the leads?
Standard ADF/XML, the automotive lead format that virtually every dealer CRM can ingest.
How are leads delivered?
MarketSync emails the ADF/XML lead to your CRM's intake email address, with the vehicle and customer details attached.
Do I need special CRM integration?
No. Because delivery is standard ADF/XML by email, it works with the CRMs dealers already use.
Is lead delivery included?
Yes, CRM lead delivery is part of the core platform.
