What is dealer group management?

MarketSync supports multi-store operators with a five-level hierarchy — group, dealer, manager, sales rep, and independent rep. A group admin sees every store's numbers in one place, managers get full control of their own store, and reps see their own postings and stats. Dealers join a group with a simple invite code and keep all of their existing data. Billing is flexible: pay for the whole group at a custom price, or let each dealer keep their own subscription so one store can leave without disrupting the rest.

Frequently asked questions

Do dealers lose data when they join a group?
No. Joining a group with an invite code attaches the dealership without touching its inventory, listings, or history.
How does group billing work?
You can bill the whole group at a custom monthly price, or let each dealer keep their own subscription so one store can leave without affecting the others.
What roles are available?
Group admin, dealer, manager, sales rep, and independent rep — each with the appropriate level of access.
Can a manager run just their store?
Yes. Managers get full dealer-level control scoped to the store they manage.
