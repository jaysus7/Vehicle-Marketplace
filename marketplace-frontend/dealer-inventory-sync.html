What is automatic inventory sync?

MarketSync connects to your dealer website's live inventory and keeps its own copy in step with it. It reads your feed on a schedule, pulls in new vehicles, updates prices and photos, and removes cars that have left the lot — no CSV exports or manual uploads. It works across the common dealer website platforms, and when a site doesn't publish a clean data feed, MarketSync loads the pages in a real browser to read the inventory the same way a shopper's browser would.

Frequently asked questions

Do I have to upload a spreadsheet?
No. MarketSync reads your live dealer-website inventory automatically, so there is nothing to export or import.
What if my website doesn't have a data feed?
MarketSync falls back to loading your inventory pages in a real headless browser and reading the vehicles the way a shopper's browser would, so it works even without a structured feed.
How often does it sync?
Inventory refreshes automatically on a schedule, so new arrivals, price changes and sold units are reflected without any manual step.
Does it decode VINs?
Yes — every VIN is decoded automatically on sync, which powers window stickers, brochures and inventory analytics.
