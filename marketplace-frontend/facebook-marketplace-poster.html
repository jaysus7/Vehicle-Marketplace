What is the MarketSync auto-poster?

MarketSync is a Chrome extension and dashboard that lets car dealers and sales reps post their inventory to Facebook Marketplace fast. Pick a vehicle, click Post, and the extension opens the Marketplace listing form and fills in the year, make, model, price, mileage, colours and body style, uploads the photos in order, and pastes a clean description. A listing that used to take several minutes now takes seconds — and you still click the final Post button yourself.

Frequently asked questions

Does MarketSync use the Facebook Marketplace API?
No. Facebook Marketplace has no public API for posting vehicle listings, so MarketSync works through the real Marketplace page in your own browser via a Chrome extension. That is exactly how Facebook expects a person to post.
Is auto-posting against Facebook's rules?
No. You still click the Post button yourself — MarketSync just fills in the form fields and photos for you, like an assistant typing on your behalf. Facebook's rules target bots that auto-post and spam, which MarketSync is not.
How long does it take to post a vehicle?
A few seconds. The extension fills the year, make, model, price, mileage, colours and description and uploads the photos, then you review and post.
Do sales reps need their own logins?
Each rep uses their own MarketSync account and their own Facebook account. Under a dealer plan, reps are covered by the dealership's subscription.
