The Complete Toolkit

Every feature, explained in plain English

MarketSync started as a Facebook Marketplace poster. It's now the full system dealers use to post faster, price smarter, track every deal, and manage their whole team. Here's exactly what each piece does.

Core

Facebook Marketplace Auto-Poster

Posting a vehicle to Facebook Marketplace by hand means retyping the year, make, model, price, mileage and a description, then uploading photos one at a time — a few minutes per car. Our Chrome extension does the whole thing for you. Pick a vehicle from your inventory, click Post, and the extension opens the Marketplace listing form and fills in every field, drops in the photos, and pastes a clean description — so a listing that took minutes now takes seconds.

  • Fills year, make, model, price, mileage, colors & body style automatically
  • Uploads your vehicle photos in the right order
  • Docks as a side panel so you can see your inventory while you post
  • Live progress bar shows exactly what's filling in
Why an extension and not an API? Facebook Marketplace has no public API for posting vehicle listings — the only way to post is through the real Marketplace page. The extension works inside your own browser, on your own account, which is exactly how Facebook expects a person to post.
Core

Automatic Inventory Sync

Connect your dealer website once and MarketSync keeps your inventory in step with it — no spreadsheets, no manual uploads. It reads your live feed, pulls in new arrivals, updates prices, and quietly removes cars that have left the lot. It works across the common dealer website platforms, and when a site doesn't publish a clean feed, MarketSync loads the pages in a real browser to read the inventory the same way a shopper's browser would.

  • New vehicles appear automatically
  • Price and photo changes flow through on each sync
  • Sold or removed cars drop off on their own
  • Every VIN is decoded automatically as it syncs
Core

Sales Pipeline & Appointments

Every car you post lands on a drag-and-drop board with four columns: Posted → Appointment Set → Claimed Sale → Need Relisting. Drag a card as the deal moves. Set an appointment with a date and time and MarketSync sends a reminder before it happens. Cars that have sat too long automatically surface in Need Relisting with a one-click repost button. Each card links straight to its Facebook post and its page on your website, and can collapse down to just the essentials when the board gets busy.

  • Drag cards between stages as deals progress
  • Appointment date/time with automatic reminders
  • Marking a car sold logs the sale & awards leaderboard points
  • Stale posts flagged for a one-click relist
Core

Team Leaderboard & Insights

Turn posting and selling into a bit of friendly competition. Reps earn points for posting cars and for sales, and the leaderboard ranks the whole team so managers can see who's active and who needs a nudge. Insights roll up postings, sales, and activity across the store so you always know your numbers for the week.

AI Vision — Photo Scoring

Photos sell cars, and AI Vision grades yours. It scans each listing's gallery and scores it 0–100 based on how many photos there are, their resolution and lighting, and an AI look at the hero shot for blur, clutter, bad angles, or a photo that doesn't clearly show the vehicle. The lowest-scoring listings surface first with clear flags — "too dark," "only 2 photos," "blurry hero" — so you know exactly which cars to reshoot to get more clicks.

  • Every listing's photos scored 0–100
  • Flags dark, blurry, low-res & too-few photos
  • Worst listings surface first to fix fast
  • Runs in the background across your lot
For Groups

Dealer Groups & Roles

Run more than one store? Roll them into a dealer group with a five-level hierarchy — group → dealer → manager → sales rep → independent rep. A group admin sees every store's numbers in one place; managers get full control of their own store; reps see their own postings and stats. Dealers join a group with a simple invite code and keep all their existing data. Billing is flexible: pay for the whole group at a custom price, or let each dealer keep their own subscription so one store can leave without disrupting the rest.

  • Group, dealer, manager & rep roles
  • One-code join — dealers keep their data
  • Group-wide numbers for the admin
  • Flexible group or per-dealer billing
Core

CRM Lead Delivery

When a shopper messages you about a car, log the lead in MarketSync and it delivers a standard ADF/XML lead — the format virtually every automotive CRM understands — straight to your CRM's intake email. Your leads land in the same place as the rest, with the vehicle and customer details attached, so nothing falls through the cracks.

Facebook Posting Safety

Post too much, too fast and Facebook may flag your account. MarketSync's guardrails keep your posting cadence in a natural range — a daily cap and minimum spacing between posts — so you list your whole lot without tripping spam limits. You can also brand your photos with a phone number or logo overlay before they go up. It all runs quietly in the background to protect your account.

  • Daily posting cap & minimum spacing
  • Natural cadence to avoid spam flags
  • Optional phone/logo photo overlays
  • Configurable per dealership
