21. Collect a deposit (Stripe, Square & bank transfer) New

Take a real, refundable deposit that lands straight in your own payout account — on your website and right inside a deal. MarketSync never holds the money.

  1. 1In Settings → Integrations → Payments, connect Online Deposits (Stripe) or Deposits (Square) — whichever you run your store on. Finish the provider’s secure onboarding, then set your default deposit amount.
  2. 2On any deal, click Collect deposit — set the amount (or use your default) and you get a secure link to copy, text, or open to take payment. Once paid, the customer record auto-stamps “deposit paid” with a “Deposit” tag.
  3. 3Shoppers can also pay a “Reserve this vehicle” deposit from your public site — it drops a lead + contact in the CRM instantly.
  4. 4Lower fees, optional: tick Also accept bank transfer on the Stripe deposits card to offer pre-authorized debit (Canada) or ACH (US) alongside card. Enable the method in your Stripe dashboard first; card is always offered as a fallback.

22. Verify a customer's identity (ID + selfie) New

Confirm a buyer is who they say they are with a real government-ID + selfie/liveness check. The ID images stay with the verification provider — MarketSync only records the pass/fail result and a short summary.

  1. 1Quick add from a licence: the Identity scan button in the top bar opens a new contact and fires the licence camera scan — AI reads the licence and fills in the customer’s details for you to confirm.
  2. 2Full certification: open a saved customer record and, under Identity verification, click Start verification. Send the customer the secure link (or open it on your device) — they photograph their ID and take a selfie.
  3. 3Press Refresh status to update the badge to ID verified (with name, DOB & selfie-matched), Awaiting customer, or Needs another attempt.
  4. 4Choose your provider: MarketSync supports Stripe Identity and Persona (often cheaper / broader regional coverage). When both are set up, managers pick which one this store uses from the Provider dropdown on the customer’s verification panel.

37. Commissions & pay plans

Pay is calculated from the deal automatically — no spreadsheets, no month-end arguments. Reps see their own; managers see the team.

  1. 1Set a pay plan — a % of gross, a flat amount, or the greater of the two — globally or per vehicle. Add F&I pay for the F&I manager, and split deals between two reps.
  2. 2When a deal is delivered (§18), MarketSync computes every commission line — salesperson, co-sell split, F&I — and posts it to the rep's My commission page.
  3. 3Commission is earned when the deal funds/pays, and can be clawed back if a deal unwinds. Bonuses and clawbacks show right on the rep's profile.

38. Accounting — it mostly runs itself

Delivered deals, F&I income, tax and deposits post themselves to the ledger. Your team only types in expenses. Every day the books check themselves and email you if something's off.

  1. 1Reconciliation — a green “Day balances” or red “Books don't reconcile” banner, plus income, deposits, expenses and variance for the day.
  2. 2Bank account — link your bank (read-only) via Plaid so the daily check runs against real money. Expenses — the one tab your team fills in.
  3. 3Tax shows collected vs paid vs owing (auto-filled from deals). Reports gives the full ledger + CSV export. Settings sets alert emails, tolerance, and the auto-post switches.
