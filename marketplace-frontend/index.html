What is MarketSync?

Here is the simple version.

Car dealerships have a website. The website shows every car they have for sale. There can be 50 cars. There can be 500. Each car has a price, photos, and a list of what makes it special.

Facebook Marketplace is one of the biggest places people look when they want to buy a car. But posting a car to Facebook takes a long time. You have to type the year, the make, the model, the price. You have to upload all the photos. You have to write a description. For 100 cars, that is many hours of work.

MarketSync does the boring part for you. It looks at your dealership website, grabs every car, and helps you post each one to Facebook Marketplace in under a minute. It also keeps score so you can see which sales rep is posting and selling the most cars.

Think of it like a remote control for Facebook Marketplace, made just for car dealers.

The Chrome Extension

The Tool That Fills Out Facebook For You

An extension is a small free helper that sits in your Chrome browser. The MarketSync extension only does one thing: it makes Facebook Marketplace easy.

It fills in every box

Year, make, model, price, mileage, color, description, all the photos. You do not touch the keyboard.

Mark a vehicle as posted

After you post a car, click the green check. MarketSync remembers and removes it from your "to-do" list so you never post the same car twice.

Tell us when a car sold

If you sold the car, click I Sold It. You score 500 points. If Facebook itself shows the car is sold, MarketSync sees it on its own. No paperwork.

Safe and private

It only wakes up on Facebook Marketplace. It never reads your messages, your posts, or your friends list.

Sold Tracking

Three Ways a Car Gets Marked Sold

MarketSync knows the difference between you closing a deal, someone else closing a deal, and Facebook noticing on its own. Each one is tracked differently.

I Sold It

You closed the deal. Click the button. You get +500 points and the sale shows on your stats.

Use this when you are the one who shook the buyer's hand.

Sold by Other

Someone else on your team sold the car. No points to you, but MarketSync removes the car from your "to-do" list so you do not waste time.

Honest tracking. Nobody steals credit.

Facebook Auto-Detect

Sometimes a buyer marks the listing sold on Facebook before anyone tells you. The extension sees it and updates the dashboard for you.

No clicks needed. It just works.

Common Questions

Do I really not need a credit card?
Correct. You can use MarketSync free for 7 full days without giving any payment info. After day 7, the dashboard will ask if you want to add a card and continue. If you don't want to, your account just pauses.
Is this against Facebook's rules?
No. You still click the Post button yourself. MarketSync just fills in the boxes for you, like an assistant typing for you. Facebook's rules ban bots that auto-post and spam — MarketSync is not that.
What if my dealer website is not in your list?
MarketSync has a Smart Detect feature. If we don't recognize your platform, our system opens your site in a quiet test browser, finds the inventory data on its own, and saves it. Most websites work the first time. If something doesn't work, our support team will set it up for you.
Does it work on Canadian dealers too?
Yes. Canadian and US dealers both work. Canadian platforms like EDealer, LeadBox, Strathcom, SM360, and Vicimus are all supported. US platforms like Dealer.com, Dealer Inspire, CDK Global, and DealerFire are supported too.
What if my whole team uses MarketSync?
Use the Dealer Plan. The dealer admin pays one subscription. Every sales rep on the team gets full access at no extra cost. Reps don't pay anything — the dealer covers it.
Can I cancel any time?
Yes. Cancel from your dashboard in two clicks. You keep access until the end of the month you already paid for. Nothing locks you in.
