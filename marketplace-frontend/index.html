How it works

Three steps. Zero copy-pasting.

Connect your inventory

Paste your dealership inventory URL once. MarketSync pulls every vehicle — make, model, mileage, color, photos, pricing — and keeps it in sync automatically.

Install the Chrome extension

One-click install. Your full inventory shows up in a popup, with photos, pricing, and a "Post" button next to every vehicle.

Click Post. We open Facebook Marketplace's vehicle form and auto-fill year, make, model, condition, mileage, price, description, and photos. You review and publish.
