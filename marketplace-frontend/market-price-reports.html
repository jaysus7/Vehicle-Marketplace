What is a market price report?

A market price report is a per-vehicle, dealer-grade pricing one-pager built from live listings on major US and Canadian marketplaces. It shows your price-to-market position, a comparable price range, mileage analysis, and average days on market — matched on the same model, year, trim, condition and comparable mileage so the comps are apples-to-apples. Reports export to PDF and cache for a week so they stay fast. Current-model-year and new units are skipped, since there is no reliable used-market comparison for them.

Frequently asked questions

Where does the pricing data come from?
From live listings on major US and Canadian marketplaces such as AutoTrader and CarGurus, matched to the same model, year, trim, condition and comparable mileage as your vehicle.
Why don't I get a report for a brand-new or current-year vehicle?
New, demo and current-model-year vehicles have no reliable used-market comparison set, so MarketSync skips them rather than show a misleading number.
Can I export the report?
Yes, every report exports to PDF and caches for a week for fast repeat viewing.
