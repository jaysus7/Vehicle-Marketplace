Running MarketSync — your owner playbook

This is your back office: the secret workspace switch, your SaaS pipeline and revenue, and the affiliate program. Dealers never see any of this.

1. The three dashboards

MarketSync has three separate front doors, each for a different person. Knowing which is which is the whole foundation.

🚗
Dealer

What every dealership customer uses. Covered in the dealer guide.

🟣
MarketSync (you)

Your private view — SaaS leads, revenue, affiliate management.

🤝
Affiliate

A separate login & site for your referral partners.

2. Your workspace switch

  1. 1At the very top of your left menu (only you see it) is the switch: 🚗 MarketSync Automotive (demo) and 🟣 MarketSync (your business).
  2. 2🚗 Automotive is a demo dealership with sample data — perfect for demos and for filming your training videos. Use Reset demo data to start fresh.
  3. 3🟣 MarketSync turns the app purple and trims it to your SaaS pages — leads, revenue, accounting and affiliates.

4. Your financials — in Accounting

  1. 1When a dealer pays their subscription, that SaaS revenue posts to your books automatically (the same accounting engine dealers use, pointed at your business).
  2. 2When you pay an affiliate, it's recorded as an affiliate-expense line — so your profit is always accurate.
  3. 3Reconciliation, tax, reports and CSV export all work the same — for your software company.

5. The affiliate admin

In 🟣 mode, open Affiliates to run the program.

  1. 1See totals — affiliates, referrals, and total paid out.
  2. 2The table shows each affiliate's referrals (and how many are paying), rate (8% · life), pending and paid amounts, and status.
  3. 3Edit an affiliate to change their rate/months/status (give a top partner a custom deal). Pay out marks pending commission paid and books the expense.

6. Grow the program

  • “Become an affiliate” sits in the footer of every marketing page → the public affiliates page.
  • Add the hook to the end of demos: “Know other dealers? Earn 8% for life.”
  • Consultants, trainers and vendors who already talk to dealers make the best affiliates — recurring-for-life is an easy yes.
