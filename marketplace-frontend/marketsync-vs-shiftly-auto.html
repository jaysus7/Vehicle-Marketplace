MarketSync vs Shiftly Auto

Shiftly Auto is a capable, focused Facebook Marketplace posting tool for dealers — fast bulk posting, a dealer dashboard, sold-alerts, VIN decoding, and hands-on onboarding. What Shiftly Auto does well

Shiftly is built for one thing and does it well: getting a dealership's inventory onto Facebook Marketplace at scale. Dealers report listing 100+ vehicles in about an hour. It includes a dealer dashboard that tracks each salesperson's posting activity, automatic alerts when a car sells so it can be pulled and replaced, VIN decoding, feed or CSV inventory import, and a strong high-touch onboarding program with coaching and check-ins. It also offers Meta ad management. If your only goal is fast, reliable Marketplace posting with hands-on support, Shiftly is a solid choice.

Where MarketSync goes further

MarketSync covers the same fast posting, then adds the tools a dealer reaches for after the listing is live. AI writes each description in your tone. Live US and Canada market price reports show exactly where every car sits versus the market. Inventory Intelligence scores each unit's health and flags hot, cold and aging stock. AI Vision grades your photos. VIN decoding extends into printable window stickers and AI brochures. A drag-and-drop sales pipeline tracks every deal with appointment reminders, a leaderboard gamifies the team, dealer groups manage multiple stores, and CRM lead delivery pushes Marketplace leads straight into your CRM as ADF/XML. It is the difference between a posting tool and an all-in-one selling platform.

Frequently asked questions

What is Shiftly Auto?
Shiftly Auto is a Facebook Marketplace listing tool for car dealers. It focuses on one job and does it well: getting a dealership's inventory posted to Facebook Marketplace quickly and at scale, with a dealer dashboard, sold-alerts, VIN decoding, hands-on onboarding, and Meta ad management. Public pricing starts around $129/month for a single user, with dealer/team plans priced higher.
How is MarketSync different from Shiftly Auto?
MarketSync does the fast Facebook posting too, then keeps going. It adds AI-written listing copy, live US and Canada market price reports, inventory intelligence, AI photo scoring, VIN window stickers and brochures, a sales pipeline with appointments, a sales leaderboard, dealer-group management, and CRM lead delivery — an all-in-one platform rather than a posting-only tool.
Does Shiftly Auto write descriptions with AI?
Based on publicly available reviews as of 2026, Shiftly Auto does not generate AI descriptions — dealers write them manually or use templates. MarketSync's AI Boost writes a Facebook-ready description for every vehicle in your chosen tone.
Does Shiftly Auto deliver leads to my CRM?
Public reviews indicate Shiftly does not provide built-in CRM lead delivery — Marketplace leads land in Facebook Messenger and are managed separately. MarketSync can log a lead and send a standard ADF/XML straight to your CRM's intake email.
Is MarketSync a good Shiftly Auto alternative?
If you only need to post to Facebook Marketplace, Shiftly is a focused, capable tool. If you also want AI listing copy, market pricing, inventory analytics, a sales pipeline, and CRM lead delivery in one place, MarketSync covers the posting and everything around it.
