What is the sales leaderboard?

The MarketSync leaderboard turns posting and selling into friendly competition. Sales reps earn points when they post vehicles and when they log sales, and the leaderboard ranks the whole team. Managers get a clear read on activity and results across the store, and reps get motivation to keep listing. Team insights roll up postings, sales and activity so you always know your numbers for the week.

Frequently asked questions

How do reps earn points?
Reps earn points for posting vehicles to Facebook Marketplace and for logging sales, and the leaderboard ranks the team accordingly.
Can managers see team performance?
Yes. Team insights roll up postings, sales and activity across the store so managers always know the numbers.
Is the leaderboard included?
Yes, the leaderboard and team insights are part of the core platform.
Does it work for a single independent rep?
Yes — an independent rep still gets their own dashboard and stats even before their dealership joins.
