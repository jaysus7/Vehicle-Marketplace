Common questions

Where do the values come from?

Live comparable listings via MarketCheck, mileage-adjusted, with a CarAPI fallback for VIN specs. Coverage can be thinner on rare trims — you can appraise without the trim for a broader read.

Does the customer see my cost?

No. Internal cash/trade ranges stay with the rep; the customer only sees the clean summary you choose to print.
